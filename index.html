About

The University of Washington is a leader in both Computer Science and Statistics, creating a unique institution for performing cutting-edge Machine Learning research. This research occurs around many areas of campus, lead by a diverse set of faculty. The MODE Lab brings together faculty, students, and postdocs in CSE and Statistics. The focus of the lab is on Machine learning, Optimization, Distributed systems, and (E) statistics (or, for Spanish speakers, Estad√≠stica). Some example topics include:

  • streaming inference algorithms
  • distributed implementations
  • models and algorithms for large-scale time series analysis
  • statistical network modeling
  • Bayesian nonparametrics
  • computational neuroscience
